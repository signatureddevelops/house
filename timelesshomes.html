Is care really free?+
Yes. Our founding covenant guarantees free access for qualifying hospice, maternity, and pediatric services, funded by donors, grants, and partners. Families do not receive a bill for covered services.
Who qualifies for services?+
Clinical criteria are set with hospital partners and reviewed by Guardians of the Charter. We prioritize need, safety, and appropriateness of care, with compassionate triage for urgent cases.
How do you protect long-term integrity?+
Guardians of the Charter, land trusts, financial transparency, and immutable ethical standards protect the mission. Major changes require independent review and multi-party approval.
Can I volunteer or train there?+
Yes. From volunteer caregiving to clinical residencies and research fellowships, the University of Health & Compassion offers structured, supervised pathways.
Where will the first campuses be?+
The first flagship is planned for Florida (108 acres), with a second campus in Trinidad (33 acres). Additional regions will follow with strong hospital and community partners.
How can sponsors engage safely?+
We provide clear KPIs, brand-safe visibility, and agreed communications plans. All partnerships pass conflict-of-interest checks and public-disclosure standards.
