About the Certification Network

The Timeless Peace Homes™ Certification & Partnership Network (TPH-Net) allows existing, licensed homes to earn recognition and funding by meeting our care, dignity, and transparency standards — while serving real families today.

Why Certify

  • Instant credibility under an international humanitarian brand
  • Free publicity and inclusion in media campaigns
  • Access to funding rewards and micro-grants

Who Can Join

  • Hospice & respite homes
  • Maternity & pediatric centers
  • Faith-based or community host homes with medical partnerships

What You Receive

  • Tiered certification plaque (Bronze–Platinum)
  • Listing in the Timeless Peace directory
  • Training, toolkits, and annual review

Certification Tiers

Homes are assessed through documentation, site visit, and KPI review.

Bronze (Provisional)

Meets essential safety and dignity standards. Six-month improvement plan required.

Silver (Full)

Fully compliant with all Timeless Peace standards and quarterly KPI submission.

Gold (Enhanced)

Demonstrates excellence in interfaith inclusion, family support, and medicine security.

Platinum (Center of Excellence)

Top 5% of homes globally — mentors other partners and participates in training research.

Free Certification Through Care-Quota Partnership

Serve with compassion, and your certification is free. Homes that commit to caring for a guaranteed number of qualifying patients per year receive free certification plus eligibility for Funding Rewards after verified impact.

How It Works

  • Apply for Care-Quota Track
  • Sign MOU outlining patient quota (e.g., 10 hospice patients/year)
  • Maintain standards and submit quarterly data

After the Quota

  • Homes meeting quota qualify for performance-based funding
  • Funding depends on care quality, satisfaction, and duration
  • Rewards range from $2,000 to $50,000+

Recognition

  • Featured in “Peace in Practice” media campaigns
  • Eligible for Girl Power mini-grants
  • Invited to annual Peace Homes Summit

Funding Rewards & Micro-Grants

Performance-based, transparent, and tied directly to patient outcomes.

Bronze

$2,000–$5,000 for meeting minimum quota & reporting compliance.

Silver

$5,000–$10,000 for excellence in family dignity & comfort index ≥95%.

Gold

$10,000–$25,000 for extended care or >20 patients served annually.

Platinum

$25,000–$50,000+ for innovation, training, or research initiatives.

Certification Standards

Every Timeless Peace Home must meet six categories of excellence:

Safety & Clinical

Licensing, infection control, incident reporting, and 24-hour coverage.

Dignity & Family Experience

Privacy, family spaces, bereavement support, no-turn-away policy.

Interfaith Inclusion

Quiet rooms, clergy liaisons, and cultural sensitivity training.

Medicine Continuity

Peace Supply mini-banks, inventory tracking, and cold-chain security.

Training & Workforce

Orientation on the Care Covenant and trauma-informed communication.

Governance & Transparency

Public reporting, board oversight, and grievance response within 10 days.

Certification Process

Simple, supportive, and built for rapid trust.

Step 1

Apply Online

Submit inquiry form, license copy, and photos of facilities.

Step 2

Desk Review

We assess policies and create an action plan within two weeks.

Step 3

On-Site or Virtual Survey

Walkthrough, staff interviews, and patient family feedback.

Step 4

Decision & Tiering

Bronze–Platinum certification valid for 12 months.

Step 5

Training & Support

Mandatory Care Covenant course and optional Girl Power module.

Step 6

Annual Renewal

Performance review, site visit, and re-certification update.
