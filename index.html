Signature D · FAQ

Clear, simple answers — so you know exactly what to expect before you step in front of the lens.

Q1What does the $250 session include? ›

The standard Signature Session is 1 hour at a single location. You receive a curated set of ~15 raws for review and 5 fully edited Signature images in high-resolution, ready for print and social media.

Q2How do I book and secure my date? ›

Start by sending a WhatsApp or SMS introduction, or submit an email inquiry. Once we confirm availability and your session type, a non-refundable retainer (typically 30%) is required to officially hold your date.

Q3When will I receive my images or video? ›

Standard turnaround for photos is 7–14 days depending on your project. Cinematic reels and brand videos typically deliver within 10–21 days. If you need your visuals urgently, you can add Priority Delivery (48 hours) for $75.

Q4Can I reschedule if something comes up? ›

Yes. One reschedule is allowed with at least 72 hours’ notice, and your retainer moves with you. Last-minute changes or no-shows may require a new retainer to book again, as your time slot is held exclusively for you.

Q5Do you travel for shoots and events? ›

Signature D is based in Central Florida and available for travel within the U.S. and internationally. Local Orlando travel carries a flat $35 fee; extended travel (hotels, flights, per diem) is quoted based on your location and schedule.

Q6What makes Signature D different? ›

Signature D sits inside the wider DeVirn Signature House ecosystem. Every session is guided by The Signature Code™ — aligning your visuals with your voice, values, and long-term legacy. You are not just “taking pictures”; you are building an archive of who you are becoming.
