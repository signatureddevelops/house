Who would be interested in the Estate Ledger™ Session? ›

The Estate Ledger™ Session is for people who already steward something tangible and long-term:

  • Olive oil estates, vineyards, farms, orchards, or similar holdings.
  • Product houses rooted in land, craft, or heritage production.
  • Family brands moving from “beautiful product” to “structured house.”

It suits founders who think in decades and inheritance, and who prefer one precise, private business conversation over a long coaching program.

What specifically does EARTH deal with in this session? ›

In the Estate Ledger™ Session, we focus on the backbone of your estate, not the surface:

  • Estate structure: how your brands, products, and entities are arranged.
  • Positioning & label: how your house appears to buyers, partners, and guests.
  • Pricing & margins: where you are undercharging, overextending, or leaking value.
  • Sales & distribution: wholesale, export, tourism, gifting, and where flow breaks down.
  • Role clarity: who carries what in the family or team, and where stress is created.
  • Next 12–24 months: the few moves that matter most for stability and growth.

We are not rebuilding you from zero. We are refining and disciplining what already exists.

What are the benefits of an Estate Ledger™ Session? ›

By the end of the session, a typical EARTH client receives:

  • Clarity on structure — one simple view of how the estate is organized.
  • Identification of leaks — where money, time, or product is being quietly lost.
  • A calm 12–24 month direction — a short list of high-impact moves, not a crowded to-do list.
  • Alignment of spirit and business — how the estate feels and how it operates match.
  • A written Estate Ledger™ brief — a one-page summary to share with family or advisors.

The outcome is not noise or pressure — it is order, relief, and a path that respects your house.
