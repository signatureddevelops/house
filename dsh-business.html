EARTH House FAQ

Clear · Direct · Honest

A simple explanation of what EARTH is, who it’s for, and how invitations work inside the DeVirn Signature House ecosystem.

Who is this service for? ›

EARTH is for small brands, estates, and families who want a website that feels composed and grown-up, without entering a long agency process. If you are a Signature D client, Kadijah’s Mango partner, Timeless Peace ally, or an aligned house referred in, you are in the right place.

What does EARTH actually handle? ›

EARTH handles the foundation pieces: website structure, basic pages, domain setup, forms, and payment or invoice links. We do not try to build giant platforms — we give you a clean, minimal, working base so your house can be found and booked easily.

What are the main benefits compared to doing this alone? ›

Instead of learning five new tools, hiring three different people, and still ending up confused, you receive one unified build that:

• speaks the same language as your Signature D visuals • connects to your real offers, not generic templates • keeps your booking and payment paths extremely clear • arrives with a short video so you are not dependent on anyone after launch.

How do I get an EARTH invitation? ›

Most EARTH projects come through existing DSH relationships — Signature D, Kadijah’s Mango, Timeless Peace, or direct recommendation. You may still request an intake using the form above or by WhatsApp. If the project is not a fit, we will tell you clearly and, where possible, suggest a simpler path.

What happens after I send the intake? ›

You will receive a short reply confirming fit, clarifying any missing details, and confirming your chosen payment style. Once confirmed, we begin setup, give you a realistic launch window, and let you know exactly what we need from you (photos, words, links, etc.).

Prefer to talk it through first?
You can send a quick WhatsApp message or email with your name, city, and what you’d like your website to do. We can decide together if EARTH is the right first step.
