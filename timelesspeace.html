Founder’s Note

Timeless Peace was born from a private grief that became a public promise: no family should face the hardest moments alone, or be turned away because of cost. This Foundation exists to turn compassion into systems—so care, dignity, and sanctuary endure for generations.

Mission & Vision

Born from love and grief transformed into service, Timeless Peace Foundation pledges free, compassionate care where it’s needed most—and builds the durable systems that make such care possible for centuries.

Mission

What We Do

  • Provide free hospice, maternity, and pediatric care grounded in compassion and clinical excellence.
  • Operate Peace Homes for respite, family lodging, and transitional care.
  • Safeguard medicines with Peace Supply Banks and cold‑chain security.
  • Train caregivers through University of Health & Compassion—from volunteers to clinicians.
  • Maintain Interfaith Sanctuaries for prayer, reflection, rites, and remembrance.
Vision

Where We’re Going

  • Flagship (Florida, 108 acres): hospice, maternity, pediatric, training center, sanctuaries, memorial gardens.
  • Second Campus (Trinidad, 33 acres): regional hub for Caribbean care and education.
  • Global Network: 10+ flagships, 50+ Peace Homes, mobile outreach, Peace Air logistics.
  • Legacy Goal: an institution designed for 250+ years of impact and integrity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is care really free?+
Yes. Our founding covenant guarantees free access for qualifying hospice, maternity, and pediatric services, funded by donors, grants, and partners. No family receives a bill for covered services.
Who qualifies for services?+
Clinical criteria are set with hospital partners and reviewed by Guardians of the Charter. We prioritize need, safety, and appropriateness of care, with compassionate triage for urgent cases.
How do you ensure long‑term integrity?+
Guardians of the Charter, land trusts, financial transparency, and immutable ethical standards protect the mission. Major changes require independent review and multi‑party approval.
Can I volunteer or train at Timeless Peace?+
Yes. From volunteer caregiving to clinical residencies and research fellowships, our University of Health & Compassion offers structured, supervised pathways.
Where will the first campuses be?+
Flagship campus in Florida (108 acres) and second campus in Trinidad (33 acres). We’ll expand to additional regions with strong hospital and community partners.
How can sponsors engage safely?+
We provide brand‑safe visibility, clear KPIs, and crisis communications plans. All partnerships pass conflict‑of‑interest checks and public disclosure standards.
