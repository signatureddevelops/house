Pink Girl Power Initiative
Girls’ Health, Dignity, and Futures

Pink Girl Power sits inside Timeless Peace as a standing promise: no girl should have to choose between dignity and basics. We start with first periods, but the vision stretches all the way into maternity, education, and leadership.

Period Dignity Kits
High-quality pads, education cards, calming tea, and a quiet way to say, “You’re not alone.”
Mini Literacy Circles
Reading, health vocabulary, and confidence-building circles for girls and their caregivers.
STEM-in-Health Pathways
Scholarships, shadow days, and mentorship that connect girls to future roles in care.
Every Timeless Peace campus and Peace Home will carry a dedicated Pink Girl Power lane — from the first kit to the first white coat.

10-Year Timeless Peace Blueprint

From One Campus to a Quiet Network

This is not a vague “one day” dream. It is a staged, sober plan: Florida first, then Trinidad, then a network of Peace Homes and partner sites that carry the same standard.

Years 1–2
Constitution & Foundation
Finalize the Timeless Peace Constitution, Guardians of the Charter, risk + ethics framework, and core program models (Hospice, Pink Girl Power, Peace Homes). Begin fiscal sponsorship and early Friends of Timeless Peace donors.
Years 3–4
Site & Master Plan · Florida
Secure land for the Florida flagship, complete master site plan (hospice core, first Peace Homes cluster, sanctuaries), and prepare permits. Build clinical, legal, and interfaith advisory bench.
Years 5–6
Phase I Build · Hospice & Peace Homes
Construct the first hospice wing, family suites, and two Peace Homes prototypes. Open limited-bed pilot under strict safety, data, and dignity metrics. Pink Girl Power pilots run in partner schools and clinics.
Years 7–8
Training Wing & Trinidad Campus Groundwork
Add University of Health & Compassion training wing to the flagship (simulation labs, classroom space). In parallel, secure land and partners for the Trinidad campus and first Caribbean Peace Homes.
Years 9–10
Network of Peace Homes & Sister Sites
Expand to a small but serious network: 1–2 flagship campuses, 6–10 Peace Homes, and select hospital + faith-center partners operating under the same Timeless Peace standard and Code.
Hospitals & Clinics
Clinical Partners
Co-design hospice, maternity, and Peace Home pathways. We bring the constitution, training, and dignity frameworks; you bring medical excellence and local context.
Foundations & Donors
Steady Builders
Fund beds, programs, and bricks with clear KPIs: families served, comfort scores, bed days, kits, and staff supported.
Faith & Community
Quiet Anchors
Host Pink Girl Power circles, grief groups, and volunteer teams. Your communities become the living rooms of Timeless Peace.
