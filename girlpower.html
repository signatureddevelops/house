Luxury with Purpose — Glitter, Grace, and Grounded Care ✨

Girl Power is a donor‑friendly, women‑led initiative focused on practical, life‑changing health access. We provide preventive screenings, mental health support, maternal & postpartum care, safety resources, and financial empowerment — wrapped in a loving, uplifting community. Elegance matters. Impact matters more.

Our Cause: Women's Health Without Barriers

Why This Matters

Women delay care due to cost, childcare, transport, and stigma. We remove those barriers with no‑cost or low‑cost services, mobile pop‑ups, tele‑care, weekend hours, multilingual support, and trauma‑informed staff.

What We Deliver

  • Breast & cervical cancer screenings
  • PCOS, endometriosis & hormone health navigation
  • Mental health counseling & peer circles
  • Pregnancy, postpartum & lactation support
  • Safety planning & legal referrals
  • Financial literacy & career upskill labs

How We Sustain

Transparent budgets, independent audits, open dashboards, and a Donor Bill of Rights. Every program has a published cost‑per‑outcome, so you see your impact in real numbers.

Programs & Services

Screening & Prevention

Mammograms, Pap/HPV, A1C, blood pressure with same‑day navigation. Mobile clinics + childcare stipends.

Mental Health & Healing

Licensed counseling, crisis lines, support circles, breathwork and stress reduction sessions. Culturally competent and private.

Maternal & Reproductive Health

Prenatal classes, doulas, postpartum check‑ins, lactation consults, perinatal mood disorder screening, OB/GYN referrals.

Safety & Advocacy

DV/SA safety plans, shelter pathways, legal aid partners, transportation & phone‑privacy kits.

Financial Power

Budgeting, credit repair basics, job‑ready micro‑skills, childcare grants to attend appointments, small emergency funds.

Pink Pop‑Ups

Community glam + care days: free screenings, wellness classes, hair/brow stations, kids corner, and on‑site navigation.

Pink Beginnings — Period Dignity & Education

First-Time Period Kits

Organic pads, optional reusable cloth pad, wipes, heat pack, mini pain‑relief kit, discreet pouch, and a compassionate multilingual booklet.

Education & Support

School & shelter workshops covering menstrual health, hygiene, cycle tracking, nutrition, and emotional wellbeing — trauma‑informed and culturally competent.

Peer Educators

Train local teen leaders to run sessions and distribute kits. Each leader targets 100 kits/semester with our micro‑grants and mentorship.

Goal: 10,000 kits by 2026 • Cost per kit: $15 • Transparent distribution dashboard updated quarterly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are services free?

Yes. Most care is free. Where costs exist, we provide subsidies and never turn away for inability to pay.

Do you replace doctors?

No. We connect you to licensed providers and coordinate navigation, transportation, and follow‑ups.

How are donations used?

85–90% go directly to programs. The rest supports staffing, safeguards, and compliant operations. Audited statements are published annually.

How can I partner?

Clinics, brands, and community orgs can co‑host Pink Pop‑Ups, sponsor a program line, or fund a scholarship. See contact below.
